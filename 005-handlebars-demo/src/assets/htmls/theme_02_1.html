Agriculture & maritime and consumer policies

The farming sector in Europe employs almost 30 million people and rural areas account for 90% of the EU’s entire territory and are home to 60% of its citizens. The Committee of the Regions (CoR) pays considerable attention to agriculture policies and the long-term protection of Europe’s rural areas. It also examines issues such as food and food safety, GMOs, animal welfare and plant health, biodiversity, health, fisheries and maritime affairs.
