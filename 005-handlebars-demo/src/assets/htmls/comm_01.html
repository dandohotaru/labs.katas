CIVEX

Citizenship, Governance, Institutional and External Affairs
CIVEX provides the secretariat for the Conference of Regional and Local Authorities for the Eastern Partnership (CORLEAP), the Euro-Mediterranean Regional and Local Assembly (ARLEM), the Joint Consultative Committees (JCC) and the Working Groups (WG) with and on the enlargement countries, the Ukraine Task Force (TF) and the Subsidiarity Monitoring Network. It provides the necessary resources and instruments for the Committee of the Regions to fully play its advisory role in the EU legislative and political process in the above-mentioned fields.
The CIVEX members and the members of CORLEAP, ARLEM, the JCCs, the WGs and the TF Ukraine, supported by the CIVEX commission secretariat, draft opinions and reports, follow up and monitor their impact after adoption, present the CoR or partner position on CIVEX work remits in debates and conferences, and meet regularly at commission and CoR plenary level or with their counterparts in enlargement and neighbourhood countries to discuss, exchange views and practices, and adopt opinions and reports.
In addition to supporting the members' work, the CIVEX commission secretariat is responsible for organising the meetings, hearings and external events of the CIVEX commission and of CORLEAP, ARLEM, the JCCs, the WGs, and the Ukraine TF on decentralised cooperation and of the Subsidiarity Steering Group (SSG) and Subsidiarity Expert Group (SEG) in the relevant policy fields. It also manages the Subsidiarity Monitoring Network; monitoring the principles of subsidiarity and proportionality throughout the EU policy and law-making process, and for coordinating CoR participation in the REFIT platform.
