Education and Culture

The CoR follows developments in the fostering of inclusion and equality through investment in education, vocational training and skills leading to upward social convergence. The CoR also promotes the role of education in combating youth radicalisation.

A new policy framework is being developed for a true EU Cultural Diplomacy. The CoR supports greater visibility for high level cultural relations in foreign affairs and contributes to enhancing the role of Europe's cities and regions in international diplomacy.
